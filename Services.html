1.Social & Welfare

FGRF Welfare Work All Over The World

FGRF has reached Palestine to assist Dawateislami's humanitarian arm, Faizan Global Relief Foundation (FGRF), is a caring helper for communities in need. FGRF follows the teachings of kindness and helpfulness, reaching out to places worldwide facing big problems, like what's happening in Palestine now. FGRF works hard in Palestine like free food distribution, drinking water distribution, and supporting the people who are having a hard time because of the ongoing problems there. By working together with others and staying dedicated, FGRF aims to bring comfort and hope to the lives of the people and families facing tough times in Palestine. FGRF is working really hard to help the people of Palestine during these tough times. They know how to help Palestine and how urgent the situation is, and they are doing many important things to ease the suffering and provide help. FGRF is working relentlessly and trying to figure out ways about how to support Palestine. One big thing FGRF is trying to do in Palestine is to make sure people have clean water to drink and get good meals to eat. They are giving out free food to help people who are very hungry and need healthy food. FGRF needs your help to keep doing this work in Palestine. Donations from kind people like you help them continue their mission of helping the people going through a hard time in Palestine.

2.MSAJID AND MADARID BY

Allama Maulana Muhammad Ilyas Attar Qadri Razawi The aim of this department is to acquire plots for Masajid and Madaris at various places according to Islami guidelines and the laws of the countries, and cooperate in their construction with the help of locals. By the grace of Allah Almighty, this department has also purchased worshipping sites of non-Muslims and even cinemas, and has turned them into Masajid. For over two decades, Khuddam-ul-Masajid Wal Madaris has been involved in the construction of thousands of mosques globally, uniting Muslims across the globe to worship Allah Almighty. We are on a mission to construct sacred places where Muslims can gather, pray, and find solace.

3.Prayer Time

  • These timings are calculated according to by default data (which is usually based on the longitude of city center and sea level means zero feet).
  • Therefore, for small cities (which are 5/10 kilometers wide) they should act by decreasing 1min in the ending time of Sehar and sunrise and by increasing 1min in Iftar and sunset.
  • Whereas in case of big cities (which are 15/20 kilometers wide) or tall buildings (which are at least 50meter/165feet/12floor or more) they should act by decreasing 2 mints in the sunrise and by increasing 2 mints in Iftar and sunset.
  • Also in mountainous areas (for 2000 feet 4mints, 5000 feet 6mints, 10000 feet 9mints and 15000 feet 12mints) they should act by decreasing the mentioned time in the sunrise and by increasing it in Iftar and sunset.
  • Attain timings of Islamic prayers i.e. Fajar, Zuhr, Asr, Maghrib, Isha and Sehr, Iftar time at one place as well as the timings of Dahwa-e-Kubra.
