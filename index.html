No Bed At Home

With the intention to act upon the Sunnah of the beloved Prophet صَلَّی اللہُ تَعَالٰی عَلَیْہِ وَاٰلِہٖ وَسَلَّمَ, Maulana Muhammad Ilyas Qadri دَامَت بَرَکاتُہُمُ الْعَالِیَہ sometime sleeps without using any mat or cloth sheet (Chadar) and sometimes uses mat made up of palm leaves to sleep on. Interestingly one would surprise that Maulana Ilyas Qadri دَامَت بَرَکاتُہُمُ الْعَالِیَہ has no bed or mattress in his home. Although when he is guest at someone’s house, he sleeps on the bed, cot or whatever is provided to sleep on just with the intention to follow the Sunnah of the prophet صَلَّی اللہُ تَعَالٰی عَلَیْہِ وَاٰلِہٖ وَسَلَّمَ.
